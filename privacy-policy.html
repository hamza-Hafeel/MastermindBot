Privacy Policy

MastermindBot - Your Ultimate Telegram Quiz Bot

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how MastermindBot collects, uses, and protects your data when you interact with the bot on Telegram. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to ensuring that your data is handled responsibly and securely.

Data Collection in Groups

When MastermindBot is added to a group, it collects and stores the following information:

  • The group's title and unique ChatID provided by Telegram.
  • The date of the last message sent in the group.
  • A list of members who have interacted with the bot by sending at least one message in the group.
  • Settings applied by group administrators using the /settings command.

All data is associated with the group's ChatID and is used solely for the bot's functionality.

User Interaction

When you interact with MastermindBot in a group or private chat, the bot collects and updates the following information:

  • Your first and last name (as provided on Telegram).
  • Your username (if available).
  • The date of your last interaction with the bot.

This data is associated with your unique UserID provided by Telegram and is used to enhance your experience with the bot.

Secondary Information

MastermindBot does not store secondary information such as profile pictures or admin lists. These details are retrieved in real-time from Telegram's servers when needed (e.g., when you use the /profile command).

Bot Administrators Access

The bot's founders and administrators have access to logs for maintenance and support purposes. However, access is limited to essential logs, such as:

  • Creation of new bot instances.
  • Requests to reset group or user data.
  • Donation-related activities.

Administrators may also access the last 200 messages from a user or group if the user or group is blocked for violating the bot's rules. This access is strictly limited to resolving appeals or disputes.

Game Image Generation

MastermindBot generates game images dynamically on its own servers. No information related to these images is shared with external services, ensuring your privacy is protected.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please contact us at Telegram support MastermindBotSupport.
